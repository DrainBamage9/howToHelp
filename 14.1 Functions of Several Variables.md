### Functions of Several Variables

Some functions are multivariate, like temp on a surface like

$$f(x,y) \ or \ f(x,y,z)$$

#### Example

Given the equation
$$f(x,y) = x^2 + y^2$$
We can determine this is a bowl shape given:
$$y=0 \ , z=x^2$$
$$x=0 \ , z=y^2$$
Both are upwards opening parabolas. Surface is a 3D bowl centered at origin.
